Tadhana

Developers:                                                      Published on: 
Project Tadhana                                                   2015

Description

A tabletop role-playing game that aims to entertain with the narrative mechanics of role play while introducing the audience the cultural aspect of Philippine Mythology.

Message Behind the Game

The game aims to showcase the theme of Philippine Mythology in the aspect of role-playing and the characters created for the story.

Testimonials

A game that brings us closer to the
multitude of creatures from Philippine
folklore and myth. It fuses the elements
of role playing games with a kick-ass
story that will give anyone enough reason
to fall in love with the magical and
mystical stories of the past.

– Daniel de Guzman, The Aswang Project

It forms the very fabric of Filipino culture,
so much so that it’s bizarre we’ve produced
so few games revolving around our stories,
creatures, and values.

– Michael Logarta, GMA News

THINGS TO KNOW ABOUT 

>Tadhana is an RPG that doesn't rely on the d20/polyhedral dice system, it instead relies on a card-based system.

>It is heavily influenced in Philippine Folklore that it serves as another way for Filipinos to learn more about our culture.

>Lahi(Races) and Professions in the game is based on various ethnicity and the world settled in Tadhana is derived from real Filipino myths and epics.

THINGS YOU NEED TO PLAY THE GAME(based on the rulebook):

 

 

 

 

RULEBOOK
Where it contains the info about Tadhana itself, the basics of gameplay, the steps of character creation, and the rules of the game.

 

 

 

 

 

 

PEN AND PAPER
Used to record or write things throughout your adventure.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

COUNTERS
These can be things like shells, old coins, buttons, or other small tokens you can use to monitor the current values of your attributes (which are located in the Character Sheet, attributes known as Lakas, Husay, Talisik, and Diwa).

 

 

 

 

 

 

CHARACTER SHEET
It contains the attributes, the stats, the traits, powers, and the items of your adventurer.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

CARDS OF DESTINY
These are a deck of cards that are used to stimulate chance and randomness in the game.
For veterans of other RPGS, it replaces the function of polyhedral dice.

 

 

 

 

"Where is the gameplay?"

Project Tadhana hosts workshops and game nights for their game for those who are interested to get into RPG and learn more about their game.
Follow Project Tadhana for updates and their group page, 'Tadhana Seekers Guild" to get you started.
